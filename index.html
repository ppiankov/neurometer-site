Neurometer

Real-time departure pressure signals

Neurometer is an instrument that visualizes environmental activity pressure in real time. It aggregates anonymous, non-personal signals to indicate where density and movement are increasing or dissipating. Neurometer does not measure people, minds, emotions, or intent — it measures environments only.

What Neurometer Is Not

  • Not a tracking or surveillance system
  • Not an availability indicator — departures suggest possibility, not certainty
  • Not a navigation, recommendation, or prediction engine
  • Not a safety system — it augments awareness, does not replace it
