Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-02-12

What Neurometer Is

Neurometer is an instrument that visualizes departure pressure in real time. It detects when people leave a zone, not how many are there. A high reading means many departures — it says nothing about availability or occupancy. Neurometer does not measure people, minds, emotions, or intent — it measures environments only.

What Data Is Processed

When a user opts into the contribution service, the app transmits anonymous departure events consisting of a zone identifier and a timestamp. These events are aggregated into time-windowed pressure signals and expire. No individual event history is retained. No data is processed unless the user explicitly enables contribution.

What Is Not Collected

Neurometer does not collect device identifiers, user accounts, location history, analytics, cookies, or any form of personal data. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no tracking pixels. When contribution is disabled, no location access occurs.

Third Parties

No individual departure events are sold, shared, or exposed to third parties. Neurometer contains no ad networks, no analytics services, and no social media integrations. Individual signals are aggregated server-side and never exposed.

Aggregated, non-identifiable pressure readings may be made available through commercial partnerships. These readings contain no individual signals and cannot be traced to any device or person.
